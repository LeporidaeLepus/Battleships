Battle Ships

   Click the board to fire the enemy's ships in limit times.
   There will always be at least 1 boat up to a maximum of 3 in any one game. The boats vary in 1-4 spaces long and boat sizes and boat dimentions will vary from game to game.
   You will get 1 point for every whole boat sank,0.5 points for each boat hit at least once (not including boats that are sunk) and -1 point for each whole boat that was not hit at least once.
